Why It Matters

Significance of Early Childhood Education

The first six years of a child's life are the most important period for developing physically, emotionally, cognitively, socially and morally.

Cognitive Milestones

Ability to think, learn, solve problems, and develop memory and decision-making abilities.

Physical Milestones

Development of motor skills like crawling, walking, running, and fine motor coordination.

Social & Emotional Milestones

How children interact with others, express emotions, and develop empathy and relationships.

Language Milestones

Development of speech, vocabulary, and communication skills in understanding and expression.
